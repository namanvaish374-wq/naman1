Technology Revolution: AI Changing the World

AUTHOR-Naman Vaish
DATE-september 28,2025

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is rapidly transforming industries across the globe. From healthcare to transportation, AI-powered systems are making processes faster, smarter, and more efficient than ever before.

Experts believe that AI will not only boost productivity but also create new opportunities for innovation and growth. However, some concerns remain regarding job automation and ethical use of AI.

Governments and organizations are now working to strike a balance between technological advancement and human welfare.

The coming decade may define how Ai is integrated into our evry day lives
